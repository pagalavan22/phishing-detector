How It Works

1

Feature Extraction

The URL is broken down into 15 features including length, dots, hyphens, IP address presence, HTTPS usage, suspicious keywords and URL entropy.

2

Random Forest Model

A Random Forest classifier with 100 decision trees analyses the features and votes on whether the URL is phishing or legitimate.

3

Confidence Score

The model outputs a probability score showing how confident it is in its prediction, giving you clear insight into the result.
